ART and MORALITY

a personal journey through heavens and hells

it all started on the 26th of Feb 2016 at 14:53

when I had to sign the university's code of conduct



How did it start?

I wondered if I could always truly commit to that code.

1

Can I ever be certain that not even a single soul will find my art disrespectful?

After considering what my art is mostly about, my answer was a big fat NO, I can NOT be certain.

 

2

What determinds the good or bad of the influence my art can have?

At this point, I realised that I'm getting into something deep. The big word appeared: morality...

3

These brought me to a bigger question, what's the relationship between art and morality?

It turns out it wasn't just me! This question has been around for around 2400 years.

And my jounery...
